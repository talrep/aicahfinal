Scoping your engagement together: Three key questions

These are the three questions we work through with you during initial scoping. Having a sense of your answers up front lets us get to what matters faster and calibrate the engagement precisely.

01 · The need

Which decision do you need to make?

Every Hymeria project is built around a concrete decision: whether to launch a product, enter a market, acquire a player, invest in a technology. The clearer the decision you want to make, the more we can focus the analysis where it matters.

02 · The scope

What is the scope of your project?

Scope drives analytical depth, lead time and cost. A few initial directions help us size the engagement precisely:

  • Geography: country, region, language
  • Market: segment, size (revenue, volume, players)
  • Competitors: top 5, top 20, full ecosystem
  • Time horizon: snapshot today, 3-5 year projection
03 · The deliverable

Which deliverable format suits your needs?

Executive leadership team, board, investment fund, operational team: each audience consumes information differently. We adapt the format to the need:

  • Executive summary note (1-2 pages)
  • Committee presentation (15-30 slides + executive summary)
  • In-depth report (30-100 pages + appendices)
  • Interactive financial model (parameterized Excel)
  • Competitor landscape mapping

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Hymeria.

How much does a Hymeria engagement cost?

Our projects start at €5,000. The final price depends on scope, complexity and expected deliverables. We bill by outcome and project scope, not by the number of days billed. A precise quote is provided within 24 hours after a free qualification session.

How quickly do you deliver an engagement?

Our projects are delivered in 5 to 10 days on average. This speed is made possible by our 40+ specialized AI agents preparing the analysis in parallel, followed by validation from our senior experts. Our proposal is sent within 48 hours and work begins immediately upon approval.

What is the difference between Hymeria and a traditional consulting firm?

Hymeria has redesigned the consulting process from end to end around AI. Our specialized AI agents produce the bulk of the analytical work that a human team would take months to assemble. Our senior experts scope each engagement, validate the results and bring what AI alone cannot provide: deep market knowledge, an understanding of sector networks and dynamics, and the capacity to turn analysis into actionable recommendations. The result: projects delivered 5x faster, at a fraction of the cost.

Are your deliverables really validated by humans?

Yes — 100% of Hymeria deliverables are reviewed and validated by our expert network (50+ senior experts with 15+ years of experience). The AI prepares the first draft, but it is an expert who refines, enriches and validates every deliverable before it is delivered. This combination — "AI produces. Humans set the direction, structure and decide." — is our signature.

What topics can you work on?

Our service portfolio covers eight areas, rolled out progressively. Available today: Strategy & Business Development, Finance & Investment, Market & Competitive Intelligence. Coming soon: Marketing & Communication, Innovation & Technology, Regulation & Compliance. Coming later: Operations & Organizational Design, HR & Talent. If your project does not fit exactly into these categories, get in touch — our agents and experts can be scoped to address most strategic challenges.

Who are your experts?

Our network brings together 50+ senior experts from leading international consulting firms and from corporate strategy teams at major groups. All have more than 15 years of experience in their field. For each engagement, we mobilize the expert whose sector knowledge best matches your challenge.

How do you guarantee the confidentiality of my data?

Your data is processed exclusively within the scope of your engagement, on secure European infrastructure. No client data is used to train our AI agents. For sensitive engagements, we offer an NDA and an on-premise deployment is possible. For more details, see our privacy policy.

How does an engagement work, end to end?

Our end-to-end process runs from first contact to final delivery in under 10 days: (1) online request, (2) needs qualification with an expert, (3) proposal within 48 hours, (4) approval and immediate start, (5) deliverable preparation by AI agents, (6) quality review by our experts, (7) client presentation (2h), (8) closure and 360° review.

Are your AI agents different from ChatGPT?

Yes, fundamentally. ChatGPT is a generalist assistant that produces single answers without validation. Our AI agents are specialized by task (research, analysis, modeling, quality control, writing), connected to more than 600 verified premium sources, and every deliverable is validated by a senior expert with 15+ years of experience before delivery. Where a standalone AI assistant remains limited and unreliable, Hymeria's agentic orchestration produces deep, robust and auditable analyses.

Who is Hymeria for?

Hymeria serves the traditional clients of strategy consulting (mid-market companies, large groups, investment funds) that want top-firm-quality deliverables without the typical lead time and cost, as well as SMBs and scale-ups that were previously priced out of consulting. Our projects start at €5,000 and are delivered in 5 to 10 days.

What deliverable formats do you provide?

All standard strategy consulting formats: PowerPoint and Keynote presentations (business read), Word and PDF reports (detailed syntheses), Excel models (sizing, scenarios, financial plans), executive summary notes (1-page executive), interactive dashboards. Deliverables follow top-firm standards: methodological rigor, cited sources, actionable recommendations.

Do you work internationally and in English?

Our network of 50+ senior experts covers France, Europe, North America and several countries across Asia. We work in English and French, and several experts are fluent in German, Spanish, Italian and Mandarin. For geographically targeted engagements, we systematically mobilize an expert with hands-on experience in the relevant market.

How do you compare to a top-tier strategy consulting firm?

On analytical rigor and recommendation quality, our standard is the same: our experts often come from leading international firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Roland Berger) or from corporate strategy teams at major groups. The difference is in lead time (5 to 10 days vs. 4 to 12 weeks), cost (€5,000 to €15,000 vs. €50,000 to €500,000) and accessibility (any organization, not only large groups). Where a top firm mobilizes several junior consultants supervised by a partner, Hymeria mobilizes 40+ specialized AI agents plus a dedicated senior expert.

What happens if I am not satisfied with the deliverable?

Every engagement includes a presentation and challenge session with the dedicated senior expert. If adjustments are required (refining the scope, deepening analyses, reformatting the output), they are included in the initial package. Our commitment is to deliver work that is directly actionable and validated by a senior expert — not a draft you have to redo. For 30 days after delivery, we remain available for questions and clarifications.
